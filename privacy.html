Uzyt is a peer-to-peer marketplace application operated by Rynos LLC, an Illinois limited liability company. This Privacy Policy governs how we collect, use, disclose, and protect personal information from users of the Uzyt mobile application.

I. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Full name, username, email address, and phone number
  • Profile photo and bio
  • Date of birth (for age verification — must be 18+)
  • Password (stored encrypted)

Identity Verification Data

Uzyt requires identity verification powered by Stripe Identity. This may include government-issued ID documents and biometric selfie data processed by Stripe, not Rynos LLC directly.

Listing & Transaction Data

  • Listing titles, descriptions, photos, prices, and categories
  • Transaction history, offers, rental agreements

Device & Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • IP address and general location
  • Search queries and browsing activity within the app

II. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you — when you register, create listings, or contact support
  • Automatically — through app usage and analytics
  • From third parties — Stripe, Supabase, and Expo

III. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To facilitate transactions between buyers and sellers
  • To verify your identity and prevent fraud
  • To process payments and manage escrow
  • To send notifications about listings, offers, and messages
  • To enforce our Terms of Service and Code of Ethics
  • To comply with legal obligations

IV. With Whom We Share Your Information

Service Providers

  • Stripe — payment processing and identity verification
  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage
  • Expo / EAS — app build and delivery
We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

V. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption. We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by contacting legal@rynosops.com.

VI. Identity Verification

Uzyt uses Stripe Identity to verify users before allowing transactional activity. Rynos LLC does not store raw identity documents.

VII. Payments & Financial Data

All payment processing is handled by Stripe. Rynos LLC does not store credit card numbers or bank account details.

VIII. Location Data

Uzyt uses your location to show nearby listings. We collect location only when you grant permission. You may disable location access at any time in device settings.

IX. Your Privacy Rights & Choices

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out — opt out of non-essential communications

X. Children's Privacy

Uzyt is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

XI. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Your continued use of Uzyt after changes constitutes acceptance.
